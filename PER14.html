Question Types Glossary

Questions at the levels below may start with the following words:

Knowledge: list, name, state, identify, find
Comprehension: explain, describe, discuss, restate
Application: build, remodel, make, write, apply
Analyze: support, refute, distinguish, identify, separate
Synthesis: build, compose, create, develop, design, revise
Evaluation: compare, contrast, judge, justify, support

To train your student to think critically, download the booklet, How to Ask Questions That Matter. Go to www.basicskills.net. Click Products Tab, then Language Arts.
